Description

This is a simple Tea recipe for Chai Tea mix.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened instant tea
  • 1 cup nonfat dry milk powder
  • 1 cup powdered non-dairy creamer2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup powdered non-dairy creamer
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine sugar, instant tea, milk powder, nondairy creamer, and vanilla-flavored creamer in a large bowl. Stir in ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom.
  3. Blend 1 cup at a time in a food processor or blender until a fine powder.
  4. Stir 2 heaping tablespoons chai tea mix into a mug of hot water for 1 serving.
  5. Enjoy!
